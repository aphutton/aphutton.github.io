Professional Summary

Incoming Ph.D. student in Cognitive Science at UC San Diego, working at the intersection of computational neuroscience, signal processing, and statistical modeling. My research investigates developmental dynamics of neural networks using human induced pluripotent stem cell (hiPSC) cortical cultures and human EEG.

Experience

  • 2024 - Present

    La Jolla, CA

    Research Assistant & Data Engineer
    Voytek Lab, UC San Diego
    Biological Neural Network Projects and Electroencephalography Projects
    • Led project investigating the influence of distinct excitatory-inhibitory cell-type proportions on the development of network dynamics in human induced pluripotent stem cell (hiPSC)-derived cortical cultures.
    • Computationally modeled network connectivity and spectral features across hiPSC early development by employing graph-theoretical analyses, spectral parameterization, and spike synchrony analyses, to reveal underlying developmental dynamics in a novel system (distinct cell-type proportions with astrocytes).
    • Developed custom data pipelines for analysis of spike trains recorded from hiPSC neuronal cultures to quantify the oscillatory effects of pharmaceuticals designed to inhibit activity of different cell types (specifically glutamatergic and GABAergic neurons).
    • Implemented and modified open-source burst detection algorithms to identify time points of network burst activity in hiPSC cultures, guiding further analysis and characterization of nested oscillatory dynamics.
    • Investigated aperiodic and periodic oscillatory features of EEG recordings from non-verbal autism children receiving FDA-approved oral CBD preparations to identify treatment success and potential biomarkers correlated with symptom severity/reduction.
    • Utilized a spectral parameterization algorithm (specparam) and linear mixed effects models to parameterize neural power spectra into aperiodic and adjusted-periodic features, and analyzed components of EEG signals across treatment.
    • Optimized data pipelines with custom Python and MATLAB scripts to ensure accurate and efficient data cleaning, storage, analysis, and visualization of large multimodal datasets.
